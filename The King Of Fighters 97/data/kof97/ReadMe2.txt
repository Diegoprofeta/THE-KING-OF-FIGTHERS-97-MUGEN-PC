 3. Installation
 =======================================================================================================
 - Unzip the files into your screenpack directory
 - Open the SYSTEM.DEF of your screen pack with a text editor
 - Add this after "intro.storyboard="
	 kof97intro.def
 - Save the modifications
 - Launch MUGEN
 - Enjoy!
