Introduction to Clark





Welcome. If you haven't noticed yet, I'm one of the (not really) numerous M.U.G.E.N. developers out there. Thanks for downloading and trying out Clark Steel from The King Of Fighters 2002 with some KOF'01 & KOF'03 stuff. These games were developed by SNK, now known as SNK Playmore. This is my first attempt at creating a character.


1. This character has been built using sprites and sounds from KOF'99, KOF'02 & KOF'03. Those are copyrighted material and belong to SNK Playmore. I do not claim property of them for the M.U.G.E.N. use. The Clark Steel M.U.G.E.N. coding files (CNS,AIR,CMD,DEF) are my property though. I would have said the same for the sprite edits but since 80 % of the work was done by SNK, I can't get the full credit for them. Of course that doesn't mean that you can use the sprite edits featured in this character without asking me permission.

2. This character has not been built to replace the original character featured in The King Of Fighters. I mean that the original character plays better & is obviously more accurate than this conversion. M.U.G.E.N. does not and cannot replace the original game.

3. This character has been brought to you thanks to the dozens of hours I spent working on him. You can understand that this is hard work, and hard workers hate being stolen. If you wish to use small portions of Clark code for your character, you can do so without my permission, provided that you credit me for what you used. But if you wish to copy a whole move / a special coding technique (I'm not an advanced coder :p) or anything that involves a big piece of code, please contact me first.  If the portions you require are ones I also took off other creators' work (with permission of course), ask them directly.

4. Do not host this character on your site or on any other site unless I gave authorization to do so. So far, ShinMugen is the only one allowed to host Clark Steel files (via Mugen DEV board). Of course, do not claim this character is yours just by changing author name as you'd get flamed & buried in the minute by 99 % of the M.U.G.E.N. community.

5. Do not update this character. This aims people who like to take random creations in order to "update" them with stupid super beams, strikers from other games and other shit like that. I am the only one who can update it.
