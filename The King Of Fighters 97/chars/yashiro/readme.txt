1.0 Introduction
---------------------------------------------------------------
 First of all, Yashiro having the ability to transform was an
 idea taken (..stolen?! >:P) from NeoGouki's Chris. Anyways,
 this Yashiro is mainly based on KOF98. When he change to his
 'Orochi Form' he has the attacks of KOF97 and the specials of
 KOF98.
